_Last updated_: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Launchbox ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our coworking services or visit [launchboxpk.com](https://www.launchboxpk.com) (the "Service").

## What we collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the workspace and respond to enquiries.

- **Contact information** you give us: name, email, phone number, company (where applicable). Collected via the booking form, WhatsApp, email, or in person.
- **Membership records**: plan type, billing dates, attendance during your active term.
- **Website analytics**: anonymous traffic data (page views, referrer, approximate location) via standard analytics tools. No personally identifying information is captured here.
- **Cookies**: small files used to remember your preferences and measure site performance. You can disable them in your browser.

We do **not** collect financial details (cards, bank info) on the website. Payments are made directly via bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, or cash.

## How we use your information

- To respond to enquiries and book visits.
- To manage your membership, send invoices and payment reminders.
- To occasionally share important Launchbox updates (operational changes, new amenities). You can opt out of non-essential messages at any time.
- To improve the workspace and the website based on aggregate, anonymous trends.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Where your information is stored

Member records are kept on secure cloud services and accessed only by Launchbox staff. We retain information only as long as necessary to operate the membership and meet legal obligations.

## Your rights

You can:

- Ask what information we hold about you.
- Request corrections to inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data after your membership ends (subject to any legal record-keeping requirements).
- Opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, email [contact@launchboxpk.com](mailto:contact@launchboxpk.com).

## Cookies and analytics

The Launchbox website uses cookies to remember preferences and to collect anonymised analytics. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or remarketing pixels.

## Children

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to active members by email and reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
